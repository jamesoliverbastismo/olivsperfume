About Oliv's Fresh Essence

Oliv's Fresh Essence was born from a passion for premium scents and Filipino elegance. We believe that fragrance is more than just aroma — it's a memory, a mood, a message.

Our products are inspired by the richness of Philippine nature and culture. From sweet tropical blends to earthy tones, each perfume is crafted to reflect the warmth and vibrance of Filipino identity.

Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or simply embracing everyday freshness, Oliv's Fresh Essence is here to elevate your scent story.
